P2P/NLD

P2P networks are known for their resilience and adaptability. As new peers join the network, they contribute their resources (bandwidth, storage) to facilitate file distribution, thereby improving overall network performance.

This dynamic nature allows P2P systems to effectively handle varying demand levels and accommodate fluctuations in user activity.

P2P technology offers cost savings by reducing the need for extensive server infrastructure and bandwidth resources typically associated with centralized content delivery methods.
